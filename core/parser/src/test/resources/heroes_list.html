Heroes

There are currently 124 heroes in Dota 2. Heroes are the player-controlled characters that are the central element of Dota 2. Each hero has a unique set of abilities and characteristics which define their play style and role in game. Heroes are classified by their primary attribute: Strength, Agility, Intelligence, or Universal.
